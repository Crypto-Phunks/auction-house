CryptoPhunks (Phunks) is a Non-Fungible Token (NFT) collection on the Ethereum blockchain. The project was created in June 2021 by a group of anonymous developers to poke fun (parody) at the high-brow, pompous group of people that were reflecting the "old-school" rules of art into this new frontier of Web3 and NFTs.

July 2021, the anonymous devs went out for a pack of smokes and never came back, completely abandoning the Phunks community. They did not transfer the smart contract, social media accounts, or the 496 CryptoPhunks NFTs held in the dev wallet.

May 2022, Azuki developer under pseudonym ZAGABOND revealed himself as the original creator of CryptoPhunks followed by an apology and transfer of smart contract ownership, 496 CryptoPhunks NFTs and $1.44M royalties to the Phunks community.

This was the result of excessive research done by Phunks community members Kiosko, Jpeg Papi, hwonder, and Pauly

Thus, a community-owned Treasury Vault was created and is represented by Chopper, Eggboi, Mort0x, Jpeg Papi, and Kenobi as multisig signers.

CryptoPhunks Auction House was built by Chopper and Kenobi with the purpose of facilitating perpetual auctions for CryptoPhunks held in the Treasury Vault. All Ethereum collected from settled auctions will remain in the treasury under direction of the community.

No fees, no royalties. Phree. Phunky.
